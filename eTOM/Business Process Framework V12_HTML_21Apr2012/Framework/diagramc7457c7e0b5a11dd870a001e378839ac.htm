1.1.1.13 Charging

Diagram Description

Charging processes assure appropriate rating and discounting of event records based on prices. Prices for products are established through mechanisms such as contracts, tariffs, service agreements or price lists.

Charging may occur in real-time, near real-time, or may be executed on a periodic basis. These processes will also support pre-paid and post-paid pricing arrangements.
