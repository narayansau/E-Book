(4) eTOM Process Type Manage Customer Payment Plan

Process Identifier: 1.1.1.11.2.1

Category: (4) eTOM Process Type

Brief Description

Manage payment plans made with the customer.

Extended Description

The purpose of Manage Customer Payment Plan process is to establish new payment plans, modify or remove existing payment plans. Customer can decide payment plans to automatically pay bills from a designated account that can be credit card, back account, third party and etc. It also enables paying a bill in installment. For example, customer can apply make a payment $20 per month for $100 bill in 5 months.
The payment plan can enable making a payment by pre-defined payment method. For example, customer defines paying mobile monthly line rental fee by direct debt and paying digital TV programmes by cash.
