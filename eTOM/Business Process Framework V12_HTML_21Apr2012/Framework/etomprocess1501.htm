(4) eTOM Process Type Analyze Product Information

Process Identifier: 1.2.1.2.1.2

Category: (4) eTOM Process Type

Brief Description

Identify new products, review existing products, research information about product ideas

Extended Description

Analyze Product information processes focus on research of information relating to product ideas and opportunities and identify product opportunities based on analysis of the external and internal information sources.These processes are responsible for analysis to identify new products as well as review of existing products.
