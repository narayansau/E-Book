(4) eTOM Process Type Handle Marketing Collateral Distributution

Process Identifier: 1.1.1.3.1.1

Category: (4) eTOM Process Type

Brief Description

The purpose of this process is to handle the issuing and distribution of marketing collateral to customer, in line with marketing campaign needs. This can involve mailshots, leafleting and displays at events, inserts in newspapers, television and other media advertising and magazines, voice and text messaging to individuals, and so on. Direct marketing and sales through shop premises, in-store franchises, etc are also included, and this process also manages distribution of marketing collaterals to such outlets
