(2) eTOM Process Type Regulatory Management

Process Identifier: 1.3.6.4

Category: (2) eTOM Process Type

Brief Description

Ensure that the enterprise complies with all existing government regulations

Extended Description

Regulatory Management processes ensure that the enterprise complies with all existing government regulations. Additionally, this process is responsible for legally influencing pending regulations and statutes for the benefit of the enterprise and to inform the enterprise of potential consequences of pending legislation or regulations. In addition, these processes are responsible for tariff filings as required.
