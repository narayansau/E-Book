(4) eTOM Process Type Manage Billing Policy

Process Identifier: 1.1.1.1.15.2

Category: (4) eTOM Process Type

Brief Description

Manage billing policies associated with customer billing dispute settlements.

Extended Description

The purpose of Manage Billing Policy process is to manage billing policies associated with customer billing dispute settlements.
The process is responsible for creating a new billing policy, deleting and modifying an existing billing policy. It is also responsible for associating the specific customer billing dispute to suitable billing policy to guide SP to settle billing dispute.
