(4) eTOM Process Type Administer Commercial Arrangement for Third Party Supplier

Process Identifier: 1.1.1.11.2.5

Category: (4) eTOM Process Type

Brief Description

Establish the requirements for, and manage any commercial arrangements agreed with, third party suppliers.

Extended Description

The purpose of Administer Commercial Arrangement for Third Party Supplier process is to establish the requirements for, and manage any commercial arrangements agreed with, third party suppliers of payment services. The requirements can include payment transfer cycle, payment interface requirements and payment methods requirements. After commercial arrangements are agreed, this process is responsible for monitor the execution of the commercial arrangements.
Note that the Supply Chain Capability Delivery process is used to deliver the commercial agreements.

Explanatory
