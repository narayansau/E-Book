1.2.2 Service Development & Management

Diagram Description

This grouping focuses on planning, developing and delivering services to the Operations domain. It includes functionalities necessary for defining the strategies for service creation and design, managing and assessing the performance of existing services, and ensuring that capabilities are in place to meet future service demand.
