(3) eTOM Process Type Determine Potential Suppliers/Partners

Process Identifier: 1.2.4.2.2

Category: (3) eTOM Process Type

Brief Description

Determine the appropriate short list of suppliers to meet the specific enterprise requirements

Extended Description

The Determine Potential Suppliers/Partners processes determine the appropriate short list of suppliers to meet the specific enterprise requirements (for example, expected technical capability, anticipated delivery timeframes, anticipated costs, etc..). The processes provide detailed analysis of potential partners/suppliers, leveraging information available from the Gather & Analyze Supply Chain Information processes, as well as other specific inputs available from within the enterprise, or from external supplier research organizations at the specific time the need arises.
The processes include any cross-enterprise coordination and management functions to ensure that the selected short list meets the needs of all stakeholders
