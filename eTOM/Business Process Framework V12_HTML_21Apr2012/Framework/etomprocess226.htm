(2) eTOM Process Type Employee & Labor Relations Management

Process Identifier: 1.3.7.5

Category: (2) eTOM Process Type

Brief Description

Manage relationships with employees at an enterprise and employee group level.

Extended Description

Employee & Labor Relations Management processes focus on relationships with employees at an enterprise and employee group level. Labor Relations processes include definition of terms of employment, labor contracts development, union contract negotiations, arbitration management, etc. Employee Relations processes cover a variety of processes, e.g., counseling programs, interface to employee groups, employee involvement in the community and charities in the name of the company, etc.
