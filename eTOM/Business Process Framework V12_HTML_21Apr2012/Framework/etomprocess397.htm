(3) eTOM Process Type Guide Billing Events

Process Identifier: 1.1.1.14.2

Category: (3) eTOM Process Type

Brief Description

Ensures that the event records used in the billing processes are related to the correct customer billing account and subscribed products.

Extended Description

The Guide Billing Events processes ensure that the event records used in the billing process relate to the correct customer billing account and products. A specific event record may be related to multiple customer billing accounts and subscribed products.

Distribution of billing event records to other processes may also occur.
