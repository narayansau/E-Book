(4) eTOM Process Type Verify Customer Identify

Process Identifier: 1.1.1.9.1.1

Category: (4) eTOM Process Type

Brief Description

Verify that the customer is who they claim they are. To ensure only one customer identity exists across the Enterprise, which can be referenced across the whole Enterprise and allows the customer to quickly and easily identify himself. Before establishing an identity for a new customer it is essential to check that the customer does not already have an Identity with the Enterprise. For new customers, Establish New Customer Identify then deals this these.
