(4) eTOM Process Type Analyze Customer Payment Trend

Process Identifier: 1.1.1.1.14.2

Category: (4) eTOM Process Type

Brief Description

Analyse trend on customer billing debts, alerting when pre-determined thresholds are tended to be exceeded.

Extended Description

The purpose of Analyze Customer Payment Trend process is to analyse trend on customer billing debts, alerting when pre-determined thresholds are tended to be exceeded. The analysis results may be utilized to guide developing billing debt policies to reduce or avoid loss of enterprise incomes.
