1.1.4.1 S/PRM Support & Readiness

Diagram Description

S/PRM Operations Support & Readiness processes are responsible for ensuring that all necessary facilities related to the interaction with suppliers and partners are ready and functioning. Moreover, these processes are responsible for the resolution of problems related to these facilities.
