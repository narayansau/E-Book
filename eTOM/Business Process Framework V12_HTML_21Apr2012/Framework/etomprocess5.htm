(4) eTOM Process Type Activate Service

Process Identifier: 1.1.2.2.4.3

Category: (4) eTOM Process Type

Brief Description

Assess and plan the approach to be undertaken for activation.
Re-used standard activation processes applicable to specific services.
Provide notifications as required if the activation activity requires a planned outage or is likely to initiate false specific service alarm event notifications.
At the successful conclusion of this activity, the status of the specific services will be changed from allocated to activated, which means they are in-use.
