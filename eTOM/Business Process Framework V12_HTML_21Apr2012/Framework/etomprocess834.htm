(4) eTOM Process Type Distribute Customer QoS/SLA Management Reports & Summaries

Process Identifier: 1.1.1.7.3.3

Category: (4) eTOM Process Type

Brief Description

Provide management reports on the status of customer QoS performance degradation reports. These processes analyze and assess the customer QoS performance degradation report status changes to provide management reports and any specialized summaries of the efficiency and effectiveness of the overall Customer QoS/SLA Management process. These specialized summaries could be specific reports required by specific audiences and/or customers.
